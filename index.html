About Me

Hello! I'm Issa Koné, also known as sly.codes. I am passionate about digital transformation and designing modern web applications. I hold a diploma (BTS) in Computer Science Application Development from the Loko Group School in Abidjan.

Currently, I am immersed in the Acredi Group environment, where I actively contribute to the design and development of the Nuum web software. This Full Stack project is an essential platform for SMEs, covering critical functionalities such as CRM, accounting management, and internal operations. For this, I primarily use the Angular, NestJS, Prisma, and PostgreSQL stack. This experience allows me to apply best practices in coding and project management in real-world conditions.

My ambition is clear: to evolve into the role of a Software Engineer. I aim to deepen my expertise in complex problem-solving, software architecture performance, and the integration of advanced technologies to deliver digital products that have a direct and measurable business impact.
